What is Feature Unlocker?

It is a small addon to "unlock" the features that were not fully implemented to Minecraft Pocket-Edition

Currunt Features

• Camera in the creative inventory

• Fully working Enderpearls in the creative inventory

• Enderman now drops Enderpearls upon death (fixed!)

• In-game music addition
